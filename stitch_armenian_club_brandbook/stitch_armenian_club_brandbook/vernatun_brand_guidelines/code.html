Vernatun Brand Identity

A space of intellectual gathering, warmth, and cultural preservation. The Vernatun Armenian Club connects individuals through conversation, heritage, and community, bridged by a modern yet tactile aesthetic.

Logo Guidelines

The Symbolism

The Vernatun logo is the cornerstone of our visual identity. It features the sun rising over the majestic Mount Ararat, a profound symbol of enlightenment, endurance, and Armenian heritage.

The circular emblem represents unity and the gathering space of the "Upper Room." The typography below provides a strong, corporate yet approachable foundation, balancing the rich iconography above.
